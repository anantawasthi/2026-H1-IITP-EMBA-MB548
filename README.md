## üìö [Course Curriculum](MB548-Curriculum.pdf)

This course explores the intersection of human behavior and technology across five comprehensive units.

**UNIT I: Introduction to Talent Management**

- **Foundations:** Evolution of theories and digital transformation's impact.

- **Data Science in HR:** People analytics, KPIs, and visualization tools.

- **Tech Systems:** AI-powered talent intelligence & Netflix case study.

- **Behavioral Economics:** Cognitive biases, Nudge theory, and interventions.

**UNIT II: Talent Acquisition**

- **Digital Revolution:** AI in recruitment, social media analytics, and remote talent pools.

- **Predictive Analytics:** Resume screening algorithms and bias detection.

- **Generative AI:** Using ChatGPT/LLMs for job descriptions and candidate matching.

- **DEI Analytics:** Measuring diversity and algorithmic bias.

**UNIT III: Recruitment and Selection Process**

- **Psychometric Testing:** Digital personality profiling and cognitive tools.

- **Advanced Selection:** Competency-based interviewing and ML in evaluation.

- **Digital Footprints:** Social media screening, reputation analysis, and privacy.

- **Onboarding:** Digital platforms and personalized journeys.

**UNIT IV: Employee Retention**

- **Predictive Models:** Attrition prediction, flight risk warning systems, and stay interviews.

- **Hybrid Work:** Managing team dynamics and productivity in remote settings.

- **AI Engagement:** Chatbots for HR support and predictive engagement modeling.

- **Strategy:** Cost-benefit analysis of retention programs.

**UNIT V: Knowledge Management**

- **Systems:** AI-powered knowledge discovery and sharing platforms.

- **Org Learning:** Adaptive learning systems and skill gap analysis with AI.

- **Future Trends:** AR/VR in the workplace, Ethical AI, and workforce changes.
